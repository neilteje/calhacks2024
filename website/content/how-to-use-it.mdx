## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Visit [acme.ai](https://acme.ai) and create your account.
2. **Data Integration**: Connect your existing data sources through our secure API.
3. **Customize**: Set up your dashboard to focus on your key metrics.

## Core Features and How to Use Them

### 1. AI-Powered Dashboard

- **Access**: Click on "Dashboard" in the main menu.
- **Customize**: Drag and drop widgets to personalize your view.
- **Interact**: Click on any insight to dive deeper into the data.

### 2. Predictive Analytics

- **Navigate**: Go to "Forecasts" in the side panel.
- **Select Metrics**: Choose which KPIs you want to predict.
- **Set Parameters**: Adjust timeframes and confidence intervals.

### 3. Natural Language Queries

- **Find**: Look for the search bar at the top of any page.
- **Ask**: Type questions like "What were sales last quarter?"
- **Refine**: Use filters to narrow down results.

### 4. Automated Reporting

- **Schedule**: Set up recurring reports in "Report Center."
- **Customize**: Choose templates or create your own.
- **Distribute**: Add team members' emails for automatic sharing.

## Best Practices

1. **Regular Updates**: Ensure your data sources are consistently synced.
2. **Team Training**: Utilize our free webinars to get your team up to speed.
3. **Iterative Approach**: Start with one department and expand usage gradually.
